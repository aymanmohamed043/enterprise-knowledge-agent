### DATABASE SCHEMA (Optimized) ###
- roles (id, name, description, created_at)
- users (id, name, email, api_key, is_active, created_at, role_id)
- departments (id, name, description)
- employees (id, full_name, email, job_title, employment_type, salary, is_remote, hired_at, created_at, department_id)
- revenues (id, department_id, month, amount)
- document_metadata (id, filename, summary, keywords, created_at)

RELEATIONSHIPS (For JOINs):
  * users.role_id -> roles.id
  * employees.department_id -> departments.id
  * revenues.department_id -> departments.id
